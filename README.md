## About Me

I'm an **Engineering Director at [Onalabs](https://onalabs.com/)** and **Lecturer at the [Universitat de Barcelona](https://www.ub.edu/)**, bridging the gap between cutting-edge hardware and production-grade software. With a PhD in Engineering & Applied Sciences and deep expertise in embedded systems, firmware, and electronics, I design the low-level foundations that make connected devices actually work — from microcontrollers to microfluidic wearables.

I'm passionate about making complex engineering accessible: through courses, publications, and open-source tooling, I help the next generation of engineers build things that matter.
